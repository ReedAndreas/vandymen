Stadings are calculated for each division using the "games back" methodology. Teams are ranked by their differential, (wins-losses)/2. Their games back value is the difference between their division's best team's differential and their own.

Stat-leaders are calculated from a pool of all batters with a minimum of 5 at bats. Within that pool, players are ranked in a specific category for each table and the leaders are shown.

In its inagural Fall 2021 season, the VandyMen League has 14 teams of 4-7 players.

Anyone Vanderbilt student is welcome to join! We have players whose skills range from complete baseball newcomers to members of the Vanderbilt baseball team.

Using an algorithm that encompasses a team's average runs scored, the opposing team's runs allowed, and a measure of a specially calculated D-value (score distribution for that team), the program predicts a score for each team. Then, 1000 games are simulated by taking a random sample from each of two normal distributions with a standard deviation of the leagues current score sd and mean team1 predicted score and team2 predicted score respectively. The percentage of games won by each team is shown on the graph.
